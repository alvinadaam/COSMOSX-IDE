How to Play

  • Open the Player and load a COSLANG story file.
  • Read the story and click choices to progress.
  • Track your stats, inventory, and achievements as you play.
  • Use the save button to preserve your progress.
  • Resume your adventure anytime by loading your save.

FAQ

What files can I play?

Any valid .coslang story file exported from the IDE.

How do I save my progress?

Click the save button in the player. Your progress is stored locally.

Can I play on my phone?

Yes! The player is fully responsive and works on mobile devices.

How do I load a saved game?

Click the load button and select your saved file to continue.
